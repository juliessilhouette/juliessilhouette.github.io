Chocolate Chip Cookies


Basic cookies, what can I say

Ingredients

1. 100g Butter

2 . 65g White sugar

3. 65g Brown sugar

4. 1 egg

5. 1 tsp Vanilla Extract

6. 1 tsp Baking soda

7. 1 tsp Hot Water

8. 1/4 tsp salt

9. 200g flour

10. As many chocolate chips as you want.

Directions

In a bowl, sugar, vanilla, baking soda, salt and flour

In another bowl, mix egg, hot water and softened butter

Mix altogether, and add chocolate chips.

butter a pan, any pan.

make cookies, in any shape.

bake for 10 minutes for 175 degrees celcius

leave them to dry until hardend

after you take them out of the oven, they will look

uncooked, DONT cook them for longer, you dont know how

to make cookies. just leave them to dry

Hope you enjoy the cookies :D
