Delicous Brownies

Ingredients

1. 113g Butter

2 . 65g White sugar

3. 65g Brown sugar

4. 2 egg

5. 1 tsp Vanilla Extract

6. 1/4 tsp Baking powder

7. 35g Cocoa

8. 1/4 tsp salt

9. 68g flour

Directions

In a bowl, sugar, vanilla, baking powder, cocoa, salt and flour

In another bowl, mix egg and softened butter

Mix altogether, until smooth

butter a pan, any pan.

pour the brownies in the pan.

bake for 17.5 minutes for 175 degrees celcius

leave them to dry until hardend

after you take them out of the oven, they -might- look

uncooked, if they are, DONT cook them for longer, you dont know how

to make brownies. just leave them to dry
