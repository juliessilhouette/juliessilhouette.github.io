Green Apple Crumble


apple crumble is a pretty simple dessert.
It'll only take you about an hour to make in total.

Ingredients

1. 100g flour

2. 50g sugar

3. 50g butter

4. 25g almonds

5. 1/2 tsp cinnamon

6. 1/2 tsp ginger

Apple filling

7. 300g green apples

8. 30g sugar

9. 20g butter

9. 20g water

Directions

in a bowl, soften the butter and mix it with flour, sugar, butter, almonds,

cinnamon and ginger

bake for 200 degrees celcius for 10 minutes ( dont forget to turn it regularly

so it bakes evenly )


Now for the apple filling

dice the green apples.

on the stove, heat up, water butter and sugar, then add apples

cook until soft (10-15 minutes)

Ta-da ! delicous apple crumble :D
