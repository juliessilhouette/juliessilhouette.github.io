Lemon Meringues


recipe comming soon :D

Ingredients

1. 125g of icing sugar

2. 1/4 tsp lemon juice

3. 4 egg whites

4. 1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions

in a bowl, mix 4 egg whites into stiff peaks.

add lemon juice, and vanilla.

gradually add in 125g of sugar.

dont do it quickly or you will end up with sad meringues.


butter a pan, or use a baking sheet.

and make meringues, i mean you know what meringues look like.

if you dont, just look at the picture at the top of the page

anyway, bake them for 105 degrees celcius for about an hour

when they're done they should look slightly brown

and should be crispy.

when they're done leave them to dry for a bit

also eat them quickly, because they'll deflate unless

you keep them in an airtight container.

Hope you enjoy the lemon meringues :D
