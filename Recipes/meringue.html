Lemon Meringues Tarts


Tarts

1. 250g flour

2. 50g sugar

3. 1 pinch salt

4. 120g butter

5. 1 egg

Lemon curd

1. 4 egg yolks

2. 100g sugar

3. 125ml lemon juice (trust me its not too much)

4. 75g butter

Meringue

1. 2 egg whites

2. pinch of salt

3. 25g sugar

4. a few drops of lemon juice

Directions

start by making the tarts.

mix flour, sugar and salt first. add the butter and

mix it in with your hands, it should look like crumble.

if you've make apple crumble before you know what it should

look like, if not search it up.

any mix the egg in a seperate bowl, then add it to the main mixture

roll it into a ball and cover it with parchment paper, and stuff

it into the frezer for 5-7 minutes

after that, use a rolling pin to roll out the dough

cut out circles, and put them into a muffin pan.

bake them at 165 degrees celcius for 20 minutes

meanwhile, start making the lemon curd.


mix the egg yolks, lemon juice and sugar and put them in a pot.

whisk on (very) low heat for a few minutes until its thickened

after its gotten thicker slowly add butter, you'll know when

its cooked if when you mix it and you can see the bottom of the pan

the curd doesnt move.

when its done, pour the curd onto the tart shells.

now start making the meringue


mix egg whites to a stiff peak.

slowly add sugar and lemon

scoop the mixture onto the tarts

decorate it however you want

bake the tarts for 10 minutes at 195 degrees celcius

when you take the tarts out of the oven

leave them to dry.

also eat them quickly or the meringues will deflate

unless you keep them in an airtight container

enjoy the lemon meringues :D
